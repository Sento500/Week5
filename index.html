Project Timeline

First Semester (1 December, 2023)

During this semester, the goal is to develop a comprehensive and detailed plan for the project, including design analysis, execution plan, and parts/resource list. Finally, preliminary construction of the solar evaporator system based on design schematic.

1. Project initiation (27 August)

  • Define project scope and objectives.
  • Organize the project team.
  • Establish communication channels.

2. Complete Conceptual Design (28 September)

  • Develop and refine design concepts.
  • Conduct preliminary technical analysis.
  • Select final design concepts and perform 3D modeling.

3. Detailed Design and Analysis (29 October)

  • Conduct detailed technical analysis of key design features.
  • Finalize system schematic.

4. Prototype Fabrication (30 November)

  • Prepare bill of materials.
  • Procure necessary materials and components.
  • Begin fabrication of the solar evaporator system.
  • Optimize the solar evaporator system.

Second Semester (1 March, 2024)

This semester focuses on system optimization, performing analysis of all proposals and validating our results.

5. Performance testing (15 March)

  • Measure evaporation rate and efficiency.
  • Verify the structural strength of the system.
  • Test the temperature stability of the system.
  • Test the quality of fresh water produced by the system.

6. Energy efficiency assessment (1 April)

  • Measure the energy efficiency of the system.
  • Determine thermal energy utilization.

7. Complete the entire capstone desire (1 March)

  • Data analyzing.
  • Report writing.
